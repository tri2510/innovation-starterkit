## Copywriting Tips for Your Colleague

1. **Adapt the Tone**: Adjust the formality level to match your organization's brand voice
2. **Localize**: Change any references to match your region/industry
3. **Proof**: Use examples from your own organization if available
4. **Test**: A/B test headlines and CTAs
5. **Visuals**: Pair copy with screenshots from the screenshot guide
6. **Simplify**: Landing pages should be scannableâ€”use bullets, bold text, and short paragraphs
7. **Mobile**: Ensure copy works on mobile (shorter paragraphs, larger CTAs)
8. **Trust Signals**: Add logos, testimonials, or metrics that build credibility
